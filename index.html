  • Care Advice

    Owning and caring for a dog is great fun and immensely rewarding. But it's also a big responsibility - on average, dogs live for around twelve years, but many live for much longer.

  • Service

    Service Dogs assist people with disabilities other than vision or hearing impairment. With special training these dogs can help mitigate many different types of disabilities.

  • Our Tips

    Learn to listen to your dog. If your dog appears to be uncomfortable meeting another dog, animal or person, dont insist that he say hello.

  • Veterinary Help

    Puppies should be seen by a veterinarian at approximately three weeks of age for a physical and a fecal test for intestinal parasites.
