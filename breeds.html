POPULAR DOG BREEDS

Beagle

One of a breed of small hounds having long ears, short legs.

LABRADOR RETRIEVER

A retriever of a breed that predominantly has a black or yellow coat.

German Shepherd

A large dog of a breed often used as guard dogs or guide dogs or for police work

Bulldogs

A dog of a sturdy smooth-haired breed with a large head and powerful protruding lower jaw, a flat wrinkled face, and a broad chest.

Puddle

One of a breed of very active dogs, probably originating in Germany but regarded as the national dog of France, having long, thick, frizzy or curly hair.

Rottweiler

A breed of domestic dog, regarded as medium-to-large or large.The dogs were known in German as Rottweiler Metzgerhund, meaning Rottweil butchers' dogs

Duchshund

one of a German breed of dogs having short legs, a long body and ears, and a usually tan or black-and-tan coat

Great Danes

a dog of a very large, powerful, short-haired breed.

Shih tzu

a dog of a breed with long, silky, erect hair and short legs.
